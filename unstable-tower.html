HOW TO PLAY

🖱️

AIM

Move your mouse (or drag your finger) left & right to aim where the falling block will drop.

⬇️

DROP

Click, tap or press Space to instantly drop the block. Arrow keys also move the aim.

⚖️

BALANCE

Watch the balance bar at the top. If it hits the red zones the tower topples — game over!

🌪️

EVENTS

Wind gusts, earthquakes and a shifting platform appear as you level up. Stay sharp!
